### What is it?
Neuro-Doctor is a heuristic analysis engine. It doesn't just show errors; it understands the "why" behind kernel panics, triple faults, and invalid opcodes (#UD).

### How to use it?
1. Open the **Neuro-Doctor** tab.
2. Run your kernel in an emulator (QEMU/VirtualBox) with serial logging enabled.
3. Click **"Run Diagnosis"** after a crash.
4. The Doctor will scan the logs and provide a prioritized list of likely causes and fixes.
