## 吼 English
### What is it?
The Profiler module monitors your kernel's resource consumption and boot times across different versions. It helps you catch performance regressions before they become critical.

### How to use it?
1. Enable the **Profiler** during your boot tests.
2. Observe the historical graph; look for spikes in memory usage or unexpected delays in boot stages.
3. Hover over the nodes in the chart to see which specific commit or version introduced the lag.
